# Wizard Tower / Chaos Cult — room key

An arcane tower abandoned by its wizard and squatted by a chaos cult. Room
numbers match the labels on `wizard-tower-chaos-cult-gm.png`.

## Wing 1 — entry (the tower's old public floor)

1. **Captain's den** (monster+treasure) — old warded study, now the cult
   enforcer's quarters. A looted throne; treasure stashed under the floor.
2. **Muster hall** (empty) — the tower's reception; cultists gather here
   before rites. Four ways out.
3. **Storeroom** (empty) — collapsed shelving, mundane supplies, rubble.
4. **Warded vault** (treasure, **trapped**) — relic stash still guarded by the
   wizard's old glyph-trap. Altar repurposed as a strongbox.
5. **Beast pen** (monster) — a chaos-warped beast chained by the cult; cold
   fireplace and scattered bones.
6. **Gate shrine** (interactive, **entrance**) — the grand doors, now a
   profane gate-shrine. The way in.

## Wing 2 — laboratory (center)

7. **Initiate cells** (empty) — cramped holding for new converts.
8. **Alchemy vault** (treasure, **trapped**) — the old reagent vault; volatile
   stores, a warding column, and a pressure-plate trap.
9. **Warp horror** (monster) — a failed transmutation specimen, loose.
10. **Ritual crossroads** (empty) — the grand laboratory, gutted into a
    five-way ritual hall around a scrying fountain flanked by columns. The
    hub of the whole site.
11. **Scrying font** (interactive) — a divination pool the cult uses to spy.
12. **Lieutenant's hoard** (monster+treasure) — an apprentice's old quarters,
    now a cult lieutenant's seat over the wing's loot.

## Wing 3 — library & observatory (upper right)

13. **Defaced archive** (monster+treasure) — the library, shelves toppled and
    scrawled over; something nests in the stacks.
14. **Scriptorium** (empty) — copying desks where the cult transcribes its
    chaos liturgy.
15. **Tome vault** (treasure) — behind a **secret door**; the wizard's
    forbidden tomes on an altar-lectern.
16. **Star shrine** (monster) — the observatory, now a star-cult shrine with a
    profaned statue and a guardian.
17. **Oubliette** (empty, **trapped**) — a drop-pit closet; step wrong and
    fall.
18. **Summoning circle** (interactive) — the planar-study; altar, binding
    columns, and **stairs down** to the tower's sealed sublevel.

## Wing 4 — deep sanctum (lower)

19. **Antechamber** (empty, **trapped**) — behind a **secret door** off the
    throne; a trapped foyer guarding the priest.
20. **Chaos guardian** (monster) — an animated sentinel statue blocks the way.
21. **High priest's throne** (interactive) — the wizard's sanctum, now the
    cult leader's throne room. The wing's hub.
22. **War-chest** (monster+treasure) — guarded armory and the cult's coin.
23. **Meditation cell** (empty) — a bare contemplation cell.
24. **Relic vault** (treasure) — the wizard's personal hoard, reached by a
    long locked passage from the ritual hall.
